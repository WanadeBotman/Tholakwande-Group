About Tholakwande Group

Vision and Mission

Our Vision

To be the most trusted and recognized leader in mechanical engineering services, setting industry standards through continuous innovation and excellence in every project we undertake.

To render uncompromised service, unparalleled client satisfaction, and to achieve fair returns on investment, through creating and delivering innovative solutions that help our clients conquer the ever-changing world of business.

Our Mission

To provide exceptional mechanical and engineering support services that exceed client expectations through innovative solutions, cutting-edge technology, and unwavering commitment to quality and safety.

To maximize productivity whilst aligning costs and integrating SHEQ policies to better performance & value-added services, mitigating risk and mutually benefiting client/customer relationships for sustainability.

Core Values

Integrity

We believe that sustainability with our clients is of paramount importance and that can only be achieved when our integrity is solid. We trade on transparency and trustworthiness to build credibility with all our clients.

Accountability

We believe customer satisfaction is attainable by a reputable service of excellence throughout all levels, and that is why we make it our responsibility to get it right all the time.

Quality

Our commitment to assurance is in rendering keynote solutions, governed by QA systems that ensure satisfaction through combining both talent and skills of our employees and the technological dispensation at vast.

Availability

We are available 24/7 and there is no destination unreachable.

Reliability

We believe through our (CCCTV) performance base we can deliver long-term value and exceed customer satisfaction.

Business Approach

Strategic positioning of quality management systems aligned with qualified, experienced technical and administrative staff at the forefront underpins every area of our operations giving us an advantage position in delivering sustainability and quality services binding ourselves to full accountability in value added services.

With an enhanced culture and practice of superior service deliverables, all our resources, time and skills are invested to retain exceptional staff retention that prides itself in continuous innovative and supportive business acumen, ensuring client liaison and customer experience is of global standards.

Our network of technicians and apprentices are backed up with the support of Area Service Managers who guide and engage with their teams. The Area Service Manager plays a vital part in our department set up, as they are also the main point of contact with the Client and will be familiar with the needs of the individual business.

Quality

Commitment

We account to results all the time, we have built a resilience approach to our daily schedule in ensuring we deliver solutions at all cost, giving you the peace of mind you deserve.

Communication

All relevant information pertaining reports, pricing, equipment, planning, processes, authorization and approvals is documented and furnished for future references. No work will be carried out without prior consent from the client either verbally or written.

Customer care

Technological change, fuel price volatility, currency instability are a huge destabilizer in the economy of any country, we are always finding new ways to ensure that our clients can afford to run their maintenance schedule without hindrances of the above mentioned destabilizers.

Time Management

We render service in time all the time, to ensure profitability both ways. Prior to any project / work that needs to be done, we always engage with the client in the planning process so as to inform and make aware to the client what needs to be done and how it will be done and the costing related to the project / work at hand.

Value

Our success is fully dependent on our team's capacity to deliver uncompromised service efficiently & effectively in an environment that stimulates growth, motivation, innovation and consistency in achieving maximum productivity.

Operation Method

Assessment

All diagnostics and inspections are carried out thoroughly before submitting a costing / quotation documentation and report so as to plan and budget for the work to be done.

Costing

After the inspection report has been submitted together with a quotation / costing report, a signed copy of the quotation / purchase order from the client will serve as a formal written consent to carry out the proposed remedial as per the specifics of the report submitted.

Methodology

Furthermore to the proposed findings, a detailed continuous routine of service will be proposed to ensure the durability of the machine/ facility in use.

Progression status

Reports are compiled continuously to assess variation, and are available to any parties concerned at request. The report will reveal the cause of breakdowns, remedial plans, developments and general findings.

Hand Over

All work completed is followed by an invoice to the client, that declares work has been completed to the specifications & we are handing over the machinery / facilities for use.

Social Economic Strategy

We have incorporated government policies in creating employment. We have adopted employment policies to create the kind of jobs that upskill, empower, and transfer skills to benefit previously disadvantaged communities. Our program supports B-BBEE in eradicating unemployment in our communities through mentorship, internship, voluntary appointments and contractual work depending on the projects that are active.

We have committed part of our revenue to support NPOs in a form of giving back to the community.

We fully support initiatives that exist to better the lives of humanity, from green building initiatives, human settlement, child protection, etc.

At Tholakwande Group (Pty) Ltd we believe human capital is the major resource any entity will require, that is why we will continue to empower, inspire, motivate and engage everyone in expanding that resource for the future of humankind and business.

We constantly invest in our people and systems to ensure we are up to date with all new products and practices within the marketplace.

Our Social Economic Programs focus on 5 key areas of our existence:

  • Carbon Footprint
  • Social Development
  • Waste Management
  • Education and Training
  • Economic Development

Environmental Responsibility Policy

It is our intention to stimulate the interest of local residents to maintain a clean environment and as a consequence we believe this will create the ability to operate in a disciplined and sustainable manner and to adhere to corporate governance, compliance with global requirements. To that end, we undertake to do the following:

  • Green Building Council of South Africa (GBCSA) - We subscribe and adhere to all green building initiatives
  • Renewable Energy - We actively promote and participate in the renewable energy sector
  • Waste Management - A proactive & reactive policy for waste management is disseminated throughout the group and sound recycling waste management practices are encouraged at all our sites and offices.
  • Safety & Hazardous Regulations

All employees are sensitized to the controlled and efficient use of all resources and especially the conservative rules of OHSA regulations, and adherence to stringent regulations protecting the environment are a minimum to executing business.
