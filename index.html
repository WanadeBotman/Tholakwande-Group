Since inception, our ambition has been to deliver quality work beyond excellence. We combine the talent and skill of our employees with technological advancements, forming the backbone of our innovative and results-driven approach.

Tholakwande Group (Pty) Ltd is your reliable partner in mechanical and engineering support services. As our name suggests—"discover & grow"—we are a 100% black-owned, Level 1 B-BBEE consortium that has evolved into a multidisciplinary provider of electrical, mechanical, and infrastructure solutions.

Our motto, "Service in time, all the time," speaks to our unwavering commitment to delivering quality services and reflects our ethos of meeting the growing demand for innovative, cost-effective, and proven solutions.
